Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days (excluding weekends and holidays) after receiving your order confirmation email. You will receive another notification when your order has shipped.

Day 1
Order received and payment verified
Day 1-2
Order processing and packaging
Day 2-3
Order handed to shipping carrier
Please note: During peak seasons and holiday periods, processing times may be longer than usual. We appreciate your patience during these busy times.

Shipping Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days $4.99

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times and costs vary depending on the destination.

Delivery Times

International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days after shipping.

Customs & Duties

Customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or fees imposed by their country.

Documentation

All international shipments include proper documentation for customs clearance.

Important: International delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. Delays may occur due to customs processing, weather conditions, or other factors beyond our control.

Shipping Issues

While we strive to ensure all orders arrive on time and in perfect condition, occasionally issues may arise. Here's what to do if you encounter problems with your delivery:

Damaged Package

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damaged package and contents.

Lost Package

If your tracking hasn't updated in 5 business days, contact us for assistance.

Incorrect Address

Notify us immediately if you've entered the wrong shipping address.

We're here to help: Our customer service team will work quickly to resolve any shipping issues you encounter. Contact us at support@yourstore.com or through our contact form.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Return Process

Initiate a return through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

Return Shipping

We provide a prepaid return label for all domestic returns.

Exchange Process

Exchanges are processed once we receive your returned item.

Processing time: Please allow 3-5 business days for us to process your return or exchange once we receive your package.
